VIMBAL: A Decentralized Publication System for Open Research

Decentralized Publication System for Open Research

Concerns regarding fairness, quality, performance, cost, and accuracy arise when science is published and peer-reviewed. The Open Access movement has failed to deliver on all its promises, and intermediaries’ publishers can still enforce regulations and profit concentrations. Existing publication platforms have several serious flaws. First, rather than encouraging extensive knowledge sharing, access to publications on publisher-owned platforms is typically charged. Furthermore, most present publication systems (including both open access and publisher platforms) are prone to inefficient peer review since reviewers are not properly compensated for delivering high-quality reviews.

A decentralized publication system for open research using upcoming distributed technologies like Blockchain creates transparent governance. The proposed decentralized network combines the Ethereum blockchain and Smart Contracts with the Interplanetary File System (a peer-to-peer hypermedia protocol) to get science published and into everyone's hands for free. Reviewers and authors will be rewarded with cryptocurrencies under the proposed scheme.

What Problems will this solve?

  • Ensures fairness, quality, performance, cost, and accuracy in science publication and peer review.
  • Reduces the control and profit concentration of intermediary publishers.
  • Encourages extensive knowledge sharing by providing free access to publications.
  • Improves peer review efficiency by properly compensating reviewers.
  • Creates transparent governance in publication systems using Blockchain technology.
  • Rewards reviewers and authors with cryptocurrencies.

Technology

  • Ethereum Smart Contracts
  • Solidity (Programming Language)
  • Interplanetary File System (IPFS)
